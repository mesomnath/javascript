## Interview Questions

**Q: What are template literals?**
A: Strings enclosed in backticks allowing embedded expressions and multi-line text.

**Q: How do you reverse a string?**
A: `str.split('').reverse().join('')`

**Q: What's the difference between slice() and substring()?**
A: slice() accepts negative indices, substring() doesn't.
